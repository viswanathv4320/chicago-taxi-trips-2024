Revenue Over Time

Fall was the strongest season, peaking in October. Winter months lag behind — fewer tourists, harsher weather.

When Do People Ride?

Weekday evenings dominate. Thursday is the busiest day — the combination of commuters and early weekend activity.

Who's Driving Chicago?

Flash Cab leads in volume but trails in tips. 5 Star Taxi commands higher fares — a clear premium vs volume split.

How People Pay

Credit card dominates, but mobile payments grew steadily through the year — from 15% in January to 19% by December.

Where the City Rides

O'Hare alone accounts for 41% of all revenue. Four zone archetypes emerge: Airport, Business, Nightlife, and Tourism.
